Honey is more than just regular white table sugar - it contains many unique beneficial components:

1. Honey doesn't splash when you hit the surface with a spoon. This is a way to check if it's adulterated

2. Look for honey from local beekeepers. This makes it easier to avoid fake or adulterated honey

3. Scoop up honey with a wooden spoon. Avoid contact with metal

4. Avoid mixing honey with liquids heated above 40-50℃

5. It's great to eat honey with fresh lemon juice

6. A small amount of honey with a pinch of salt before workout can boost energy

7. If you drink milk, it's great to mix it with honey before going to bed

8. Honey varies in color, smell, and flavour - there is no single "true" type

9. Walnuts soaked in water and served with honey are heavenly

10. A mixture of fresh ginger and lemon juice, plenty of honey, a pinch of red pepper, and a visit to the bathhouse - that's pure pleasure
